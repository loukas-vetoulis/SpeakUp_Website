SpeakUP AUEB GenZ is a non-profit student initiative established at the Athens University of Economics and Business in 2025. Our primary mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and professional practice in the realm of Soft Skills.

Operating under the philosophy "by students, for students," we focus on the substantive cultivation of Public Speaking and Business Communications. Through experiential workshops and specialized thematic cycles, we provide students the opportunity to practice in real-time, experiment with their speech, and gain confidence.

Drawing inspiration from international standards—such as The Moth and Harvard Kennedy School’s Public Narrative—we adapt these frameworks to the student reality. Our goal is to offer essential tools that will accompany every student throughout their studies and into their first professional steps.
